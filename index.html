The Singing Rivers is a project that reclaims marginal data streams when generating synthesized imaginaries that digitally long for a way of life closer to nature, a contradiction that ultimately unveils its irreconcilability with the artificial realm as we conceive it.

Datasets are recolonized hoping to override yet certainly transforming the main-stream, fluent again in the natural polyphony of a complex human story.
